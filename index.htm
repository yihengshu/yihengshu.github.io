About Me

I'm currently a second-year master student in Websoft Research Group, Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Yuzhong Qu.

I’m also an intern of MSRA KC group since Feb. 2022, advised by associate researcher Zhiwei Yu.

I received my B.Sci. degree in Software College of Northeastern University, Shenyang, China in 2020. I was advised by Prof. Guibing Guo and Associate Prof. Gang Wu in succession.

My research interests include question answering (QA) and knowledge graph (KG).

I serve as a member of MLNLP, which is a large AI community in China. Our recent works include top conference group and paper writing tips.
